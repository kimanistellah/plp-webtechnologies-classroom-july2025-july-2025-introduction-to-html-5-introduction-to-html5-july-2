"question the quietness of the cosmos"

Why does the void hum so low,
as if it buried its voice in snow?
Galaxies swirl, yet hush remains-
no welcome note, no cosmic refrain.
Do distant minds sleep, or hide, or watch?
Or has silence outlived every spoken touch?
We scan with light, we beam with song,
but quiet answers all along.
So we question not just stars, but fate-
is quiet a warning...or a gate?
